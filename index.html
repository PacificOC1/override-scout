🤖 Autonomous Win Point (AWP) Logic

To earn the Autonomous Win Point, an alliance must have at least 7 Pins scored in their respective goals at the conclusion of the Autonomous Period.

📌 Pre-Scored State: 2 Pins are pre-arranged in the 2 Alliance Goals (2/7 Req met).
🚀 Scouting Target: The robot must introduce and score +5 more Pins during Autonomous.
